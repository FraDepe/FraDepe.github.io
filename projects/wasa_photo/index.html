Introduction

This social was created as a project for the "Programmazione web" course of Computer Science at my university.

Features

  • Create and manage an online account where is possible to upload photo.
  • Follow, unfollow, ban, unban an user.
  • Search for an user by nickname and view his photo stream.
  • Every photo can be liked, unliked, commented and uncommented.
